Scientific Technology

“Tomorrows Analytical Solutions... Delivered Today”

The Scientific Technology Division at Al-Essa is an industry leader in providing technology solutions to a diverse set of organizations through activities such as installing, commissioning, running performance checks and training end users on routine operations before handing over of projects.

Testimony to this holistic approach is shown by the range of domain to which we cater to, Life Sciences, Hydrocarbon Processing Industries (HPI), and Electron Microscopy being a few.

The Scientific Technology team offers a unique blend of technology professionals, product specialists, application specialists as well as business analysts who are trained at manufacturer facilities to excel in bringing state-of-the-art approaches and fresh collaborative insights to deliver a superior customer experience.

Scientific Technology represents a number of international solution providers engaged in developing technology products and are committed to bring in latest innovations to customers in line with our vision of delivering tomorrow's solutions today.
